Innovative climate practices

Smart practices

We are an organization that identifies and develops appropriate innovative climate smart practices and builds sustainable and cost effective operational solutions for implementation

Goal of the challenge

To directly promote local products, services and solutions that will make it easier for indivivuals to live smarter and more sustainable lifestyles

At the 5th Global YES Rework the World Summit 2010 in Sweden, over 150 replicable projects were showcased in the themes of People, Water, Energy, Cities and Land highlighting the great potential benefits from sharing and scaling best practices. Green Teams approach was introduced to understand how to bring together different people and projects to collaborate for a shared purpose, to learn and to scale solutions.
