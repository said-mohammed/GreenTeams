Green Trees Solutions

Our primary goal for the Green Trees Solutions is to inspire the planting of at least 10 Million indigenous endemic and fruit trees across Kenya by 2033.

Our primary goal for the Green Trees Solutions is to inspire the planting of at least 10 Million indigenous, endemic and fruit trees across Kenya by 2033.

In order to achieve this goal, a team of forestry, agro -forestry and climate experts will guide the solution. Tree Nurseries will be established in 20 Counties across the country. Saplings of carefully selected tree species will be raised in the nurseries. The Green Teams in schools will be our primary locations where students will be taught on the importance of growing trees and increasing the forest cover across the country.

Action Plan

The main action plan in the campaign is to focus on Health, Nutrition, Energy Conversion, Habitat Safety, Immobilization, (dust absorption) phytostabilization (Absorption of metals in soils) and infiltration of rain water.

Take part in greening program

You can take part in our greening forest cover solution by volunteering to plant trees, adopting a and taking care of a growing tree or donating so that we can buy more seeds and seedlings to plant across the country
